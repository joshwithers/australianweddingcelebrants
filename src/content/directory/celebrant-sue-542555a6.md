Sue Raward is a Commonwealth-authorised marriage celebrant based on the Gold Coast, bringing over a decade of experience and more than 800 celebrated weddings to her craft. As the founder of Celebrant Sue, she has established herself as one of the region's most sought-after celebrants—known for creating ceremonies that are deeply personalised, genuinely warm, and genuinely fun.

**A Celebration That Begins at the Altar**

Sue's philosophy is simple: the party shouldn't wait until the reception. Too many couples endure a ceremony that feels like an obligation to get through before the "real" festivities begin. Sue takes a different approach entirely. By creating ceremonies that are engaging, meaningful, and infused with personality and humour, she ensures that your guests are already smiling, laughing, and emotionally invested from the moment you step forward. This sets the tone for your entire day and creates a genuine sense of community and joy around your union.

**Deeply Personalised to Your Love Story**

Every relationship is unique, and Sue specialises in crafting ceremonies that authentically reflect *your* love story, not a generic template. She doesn't simply tick boxes—she takes the time to truly get to know you both. During thorough consultations, she asks the right questions and listens carefully, learning about your journey together, your values, your sense of humour, and the moments that define your connection. She explores not just the facts of how you met, but the emotions and shared experiences that make your relationship special. This meticulous attention to detail means your ceremony becomes a genuine celebration of your love, delivered with confidence and the utmost professionalism.

Sue also offers guidance on other key elements of your day, including help with writing your personal vows, suggestions for meaningful wedding songs and poems, and advice on ceremony venues across the Gold Coast, Tweed Coast, and surrounding regions.

**Professional Warmth and Authentic Humour**

What truly sets Sue apart is her rare ability to balance genuine professionalism with approachability. She takes her Commonwealth-authorised responsibilities seriously, ensuring every legal and ceremonial element is conducted with dignity and correct procedure. Yet at the same time, she creates an atmosphere that feels relaxed and authentic rather than stuffy or formal.

Sue loves a laugh, and she knows how to inject warmth and humour into proceedings in a way that feels natural and appropriate. Whether it's a witty observation, light-hearted banter, or simply her genuine enthusiasm for celebrating your day, her presence puts everyone at ease. Couples and their guests often remark that they felt genuinely moved *and* entertained throughout the ceremony—a rare combination that speaks to Sue's skill in balancing emotional resonance with real connection.

**Experienced Across Every Style and Scenario**

With over 800 weddings celebrated, Sue has encountered every possible scenario imaginable—from intimate backyard gatherings to lavish beachside celebrations, from traditional ceremonies to modern, unconventional approaches. This extensive experience means she's prepared, flexible, and able to handle any questions or last-minute adjustments with calm professionalism.

**Beyond Weddings: Your Celebrant for Life's Milestones**

While marriage ceremonies are her specialty, Sue also conducts commitment ceremonies, vow renewals, baby naming ceremonies, and acts as Master of Ceremonies for events such as presentations, contests, parades, and fundraisers. No matter the occasion, she brings the same warmth, professionalism, and personal touch to every event.

**Serving the Gold Coast, Byron Bay, Brisbane, and Beyond**

Based on the beautiful Gold Coast, Sue is ideally positioned to work with couples planning ceremonies anywhere across the region—including Tamborine Mountain, Scenic Rim, Tweed Coast, Byron Bay, Brisbane, and beyond. She travels Australia-wide and internationally, making her available for couples planning ceremonies anywhere in the country or overseas.
