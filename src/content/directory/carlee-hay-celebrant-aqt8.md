Carlee Hay is an authorised marriage celebrant based in the Noosa Hinterland, bringing warmth, authenticity and contemporary flair to ceremonies across the Sunshine Coast, Brisbane and beyond. With a genuine passion for celebrating love and connection, Carlee specialises in creating rich, meaningful ceremonies that authentically reflect each couple's unique love story.

## A Modern Approach to Ceremonial Celebration

Carlee's philosophy is straightforward yet powerful: your wedding isn't just an event—it's the beginning of your lives together. This belief shapes everything she does. She understands that choosing a marriage celebrant is just as important as selecting your wedding dress or suit, because your celebrant is the person standing before you, your partner, and everyone you love most, on the most important day of your life. They need to be the right fit.

Carlee specialises in creating ceremonies that feel relaxed, stylish and full of genuine connection—the kind of celebrations where spontaneous, magic-filled moments become the memories you treasure forever. Her modern ceremonies combine heart, humour and just the right amount of magic, ensuring your special day feels unmistakably like you: warm, beautifully personal, and unforgettable for all the right reasons.

Every couple has a different story to tell, and Carlee's strength lies in her ability to truly listen and understand what matters most to you. She works closely with couples throughout the planning process, providing clarity, warmth and genuine care at every step. Her meticulous attention to detail and collaborative approach ensure that your ceremony isn't a generic script, but a bespoke celebration that captures the essence of your relationship.

Whether you're exchanging vows in the lush surrounds of a Sunshine Coast beach, beneath the hinterland trees, or in the vibrant energy of Brisbane, Carlee crafts a ceremony that resonates with you and captivates your guests.
