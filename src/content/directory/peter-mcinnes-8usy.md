**With a rich history as a religious celebrant since 1999**, I bring a wealth of experience and a personal touch to each ceremony I conduct. Based in the vibrant city of [Brisbane](https://www.celebrantpetermcinnes.com.au/), my services extend across the stunning locales of the Gold Coast, Gold Coast Hinterland, and the picturesque Sunshine Coast. Renowned for being approachable and professional, I have a reputation for excellence that has made me highly sought-after in the wedding industry.

**My expertise spans Anglican, Civil, and modern Christian ceremonies**, ensuring that every couple's unique preferences and traditions are honoured. As a qualified Workplace Trainer since 2000, I have the privilege of training other ministers in the art of conducting services, sharing my passion and dedication to crafting memorable and meaningful ceremonies.

**Central to my practice is the use of the Australian Prayer Book 1978**, which offers a dignified religious service in contemporary English. This ensures that the ceremony is both timeless and personal, with the flexibility to tailor the service to perfectly suit the couple's vision. Whether you are seeking a [Celebrant](https://www.celebrantpetermcinnes.com.au/) for a traditional ceremony or a modern twist, my commitment is to provide a seamless and heartfelt experience that you and your guests will cherish.
