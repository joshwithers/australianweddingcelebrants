Liz Taylor is an authorised Commonwealth celebrant based in Balgowlah on Sydney's Northern Beaches, bringing nearly 30 years of professional entertainment experience to every ceremony she creates. With over 100 weddings and countless life celebrations under her belt, Liz understands that no two ceremonies are ever the same—and that's exactly what makes each one special.

## Her approach

Liz's style is distinctly personal, memorable, fun and respectful. She believes that life's milestones deserve more than just traditional formalities. Whether you're planning a wedding, commitment ceremony, funeral, naming ceremony or renewal of vows, Liz works collaboratively with you to craft something uniquely yours—something that reflects who you really are and what matters most to you.

Having spent decades as a professional entertainer, Liz is refreshingly open to doing things differently. She actively encourages couples and families to think outside the square and challenge what "should" happen at a ceremony. Want to walk down the aisle to your favourite song instead of the traditional march? Absolutely. Hoping your beloved dog can be the ring bearer? She's done it before, several times. Liz listens carefully to understand your wishes, gets to know you as people, and then brings your vision to life with creativity and care.

## Services and expertise

As a Commonwealth authorised celebrant, Liz handles all the legal and official requirements with professionalism and care. You can rest assured that the paperwork and formalities are taken care of seamlessly, leaving you free to focus on the personal vision and emotional heart of your ceremony. She's equally comfortable and experienced officiating same-sex marriages, commitment ceremonies and all forms of celebration.

Whether you're planning a wedding, commitment ceremony, funeral, naming ceremony or renewal of vows, Liz tailors her approach to suit your needs. Her style is flexible and collaborative, and she's experienced at navigating the logistics too—whether distance is a concern or you need support working through the details, she'll adapt her process to make things as easy and enjoyable as possible.

## Where she travels

Based on Sydney's Northern Beaches near Manly, Liz regularly travels throughout the region including the Hunter Valley, Southern Highlands and Blue Mountains. She's equally happy to travel anywhere across Australia for your special occasion. No matter where your celebration takes place, she brings the same level of warmth, professionalism and creative energy.

## What to expect

Couples and families working with Liz can expect genuine warmth, professional expertise and a collaborative creative approach. Her ceremonies don't just tick the boxes—they're ones that you, your partner and your guests will remember with real fondness for years to come. From the initial consultation through to the big day, Liz's focus is on creating something meaningful that celebrates you as a couple or family, while handling all the official requirements with seamless professionalism.
