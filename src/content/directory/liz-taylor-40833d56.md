Liz Taylor is an authorised Commonwealth celebrant based in Balgowlah on Sydney's Northern Beaches, bringing nearly 30 years of professional entertainment experience to every ceremony she creates. With over 100 weddings and countless life celebrations under her belt, Liz understands that no two ceremonies are ever the same—and that's exactly what makes each one special.

Liz's approach is distinctly personal, memorable, fun and respectful. She believes that life's milestones deserve more than just the traditional formalities. Whether you're planning a wedding, commitment ceremony, funeral, naming ceremony or renewal of vows, Liz works with you to craft something uniquely yours—something that reflects who you really are and what matters most to you.

Having spent decades as a professional entertainer, Liz is refreshingly open to doing things differently. She encourages couples and families to think outside the square and challenge what "should" happen at a ceremony. Want to walk down the aisle to your favourite song instead of the traditional march? Absolutely. Hoping your beloved dog can be the ring bearer? Done it before. Planning a funeral that celebrates the good times and the quirks of the person you've lost? She's passionate about creating spaces where happy tears and warm hearts are not just allowed—they're embraced. At a recent funeral, guests practically formed a kick line to Frank Sinatra's 'New York New York' because that's what the deceased loved. That's the kind of meaningful, joyful farewell Liz specialises in.

As a Commonwealth authorised celebrant, Liz handles all the legal and official requirements with professionalism and care. You can rest assured that the paperwork and formalities are taken care of seamlessly, leaving you free to focus on the personal vision and emotional heart of your ceremony. She's equally comfortable officiating same-sex marriages, commitment ceremonies and all forms of celebration.

Liz's style is flexible and collaborative. She listens carefully to understand your wishes, gets to know you as people, and then works with you to bring your vision to life. She's experienced at navigating the logistics too—whether distance is a concern or you need support working through the details, she'll adapt her approach to make the process as easy and enjoyable as possible.

Based on Sydney's Northern Beaches near Manly, Liz travels throughout the region including the Hunter Valley, Southern Highlands and Blue Mountains. With her famous pink briefcase in hand, she's happy to travel anywhere across Australia for your special occasion. Her genuine warmth, professional expertise and creative approach mean your ceremony won't just tick the boxes—it will be one that you, your partner and your guests remember with real fondness for years to come.
