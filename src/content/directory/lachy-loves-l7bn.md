**Lachy Loves – Brisbane Marriage Celebrant & Wedding Content Creator for Modern, Inclusive, and Joyful Ceremonies**

Hi, I’m Lachy – a Brisbane-based marriage celebrant and content creator with a fervour for love, storytelling, and meaningful connections. I am the heart and soul behind [Lachy Loves](https://lachyloves.com), a contemporary wedding celebrancy and creative content brand dedicated to helping couples celebrate their unique love stories through unforgettable, inclusive, and joy-filled ceremonies.

I specialise in non-traditional weddings, queer weddings, elopements, micro weddings, and personalised ceremonies that truly embody the essence of each couple I work with. As a young, modern, queer-friendly celebrant based in Meanjin (Brisbane), I am committed to creating safe and affirming spaces where love is celebrated in all its beautiful forms – whether it be LGBTQIA+ weddings, multicultural ceremonies, interfaith unions, or anything in between.

If you're on the hunt for a Brisbane marriage celebrant who is creative, warm, professional, and fun – look no further. I collaborate closely with couples to craft bespoke wedding ceremonies that resonate with a relaxed, heartfelt, and authentically you vibe. Your love story is the heartbeat of your special day, and I strive to ensure it’s conveyed in a manner that is meaningful, inclusive, and brimming with positivity.

In addition to my celebrant services, I am a wedding content creator, joining forces with wedding vendors, photographers, florists, stylists, and creative brands to produce intentional, engaging, and story-driven content for social media, campaigns, styled shoots, and beyond. I revel in showcasing the enchantment of weddings and assisting creatives in the wedding space to breathe life into their brand and narrative through exquisitely crafted content.

I am here to infuse your wedding day with love, energy, and story-driven magic – and beyond. Whether you need a celebrant who truly understands you, or a content creator to illuminate your brand or special day, let’s craft something extraordinary together.
