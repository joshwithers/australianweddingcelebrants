**At Julia Hern Celebrant, the "point of difference" is you.** I don't confine myself to a single "type" of celebrant. My diverse background and adaptable approach mean I can support just about anyone in creating the ceremony they truly want.

**Whether it's black-tie elegance at MONA or cowboy boots on a cricket pitch, I've done it all.** My couples have experienced a variety of unique weddings, from eloping on the viewing deck at Cradle Mountain to arriving on farm machinery. They've tied the knot at a cemetery, signed their paperwork over a family lunch at home, and stood barefoot on the beach to say their vows. And yes, there was that one time I kissed the best man—but in my defence, he was a dog in a bow tie, and he kissed me first!

**My role is to help you create a ceremony that not only meets your expectations but also surprises you with little touches you hadn’t even thought of.** My job is to make you feel fantastic while making it all look effortless. Since I started marrying couples in 2019, I can honestly say I’ve never had a "difficult" client. That’s not luck; that’s great teamwork.

**My goal is to create an event that is perfect for you,** so I offer three packages, priced to reflect the value of the inclusions, such as a rehearsal or use of my sound system. And if you still want something in between, I can customise a package just for you.

**Based in the Huon Valley, I've officiated weddings across Tasmania and even ventured to the mainland.** If you're looking for a [celebrant](https://www.juliahern.com) you can trust, not only with the important legal administration but also the creative execution, I might be the one for you.
