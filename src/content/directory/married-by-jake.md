## Comprehensive Services

As a Commonwealth authorised civil marriage celebrant, Jake offers a full range of services tailored to different celebration styles:

- **Wedding Ceremonies**: From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, Jake brings the same level of care and creativity to every ceremony size.
- **Master of Ceremonies**: His warm, engaging style makes him equally excellent as an MC, seamlessly guiding your celebration with genuine enthusiasm.
- **Destination Elopements**: Whether you're eloping nationally or internationally, Jake specialises in creating meaningful ceremonies away from home.
- **Faith-Inclusive & Secular Ceremonies**: Jake welcomes couples of all backgrounds and is experienced in both secular and faith-inclusive celebrations, including Christian ceremonies.
- **Wedding Readings**: Professional delivery of readings that brings additional meaning to your ceremony.
