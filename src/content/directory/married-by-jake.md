**Jake Smith** is one of Australia's most experienced and highly reviewed wedding celebrants, operating as [Married by Jake](https://marriedbyjake.com) from Brisbane. With over 15 years of experience and more than 2,200 weddings under his belt, Jake has built an exceptional reputation for creating warm, personal, and legally valid ceremonies.

**A proven track record of excellence.** With 500+ five-star reviews and a perfect 5/5 average rating, Jake's commitment to every couple is reflected in the consistent praise from the thousands of people he's celebrated. His approach centres on personalised storytelling, guest inclusion, and a calm, professional presence from first meeting through to the ceremony itself.

**Available across Australia's east coast,** Jake is based in Brisbane but regularly travels to Sydney, Byron Bay, the Gold Coast, and the Sunshine Coast. He's also available for destination elopements nationally and internationally. Whether it's an intimate elopement or a grand celebration, Jake brings the same level of care and professionalism to every ceremony.

**Jake welcomes couples of all backgrounds** and is experienced in both secular and faith-inclusive ceremonies. His services include wedding ceremonies, Master of Ceremonies roles, destination elopements, and funeral celebrant services. As a Commonwealth authorised civil marriage celebrant, Jake combines legal expertise with genuine warmth and creativity.
