With a decade of weddings and hundreds of ceremonies under her belt, Annie has built a reputation for creating unique, individualised celebrations filled with fun, personality, and genuine heart.

**More than just a celebrant.** Annie is a multi-talented wedding professional — celebrant, MC, DJ, and singer — which means she brings an unmatched energy and understanding of how a wedding day flows. Her ability to read a room and adapt in the moment has made her a favourite among couples who want their ceremony to feel as exciting and personal as the rest of their day.

**Based on the Gold Coast and available worldwide,** Annie regularly performs ceremonies across Southeast Queensland, Byron Bay, and beyond. She's delivered ceremonies globally, bringing her distinctive style wherever love takes her. Whether it's an intimate elopement or a full-scale celebration, Annie crafts every ceremony to reflect the unique story of the couple.

**Annie's approach is refreshingly authentic.** She's been described as "crackpot-funny, sharp as a sideways glance from Fabio" — and her five-star ratings across Google and Facebook speak to the consistent quality and joy she brings to every ceremony. For couples who want a celebrant who breaks the mould and makes their ceremony genuinely unforgettable, Annie is in a league of her own.
