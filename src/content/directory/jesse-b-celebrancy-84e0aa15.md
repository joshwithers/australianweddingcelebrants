Jesse Burford is a Commonwealth authorised Marriage Celebrant and Master of Ceremonies based in Perth, bringing over 17 years of performance experience and more than 150 weddings as a dedicated celebrant to every celebration he leads. With a background that includes 750+ weddings through the Perth Marriage Office, Jesse has honed a distinctive approach that transforms the ceremony from a formal obligation into a genuinely enjoyable highlight of your wedding day.

As the face many will recognise from Married At First Sight 2023, Jesse brings natural charisma and authenticity to every event. His popularity is reflected not just in his on-screen presence, but in the glowing feedback from couples and guests who've experienced his ceremonies firsthand. He's passionate about creating weddings that feel memorable, modern, and masterful—ceremonies that work beautifully from start to finish.

Jesse specialises in relaxed, interactive ceremonies that keep guests engaged and entertained without ever losing structure or heart. He believes that wedding ceremonies don't have to feel awkward or drawn out. Instead, his approach is to take the pressure off you as a couple, keep everything flowing naturally, and create an experience that's easy to be part of and genuinely enjoyable to watch. Whether your wedding is refined, raunchy, or somewhere in between, Jesse encourages you and your guests to be 100% yourselves.

His ceremonies are characterised by balanced, wholesome fun with genuine loving energy. They're funny without being crass, engaging without being cringe, and always underpinned by quality and authenticity. Jesse paces everything carefully—no awkward pauses, no dragging moments—so your guests stay involved and attentive throughout. The result is a ceremony that sets the perfect tone for the rest of your celebration and leaves everyone smiling as you head into the evening.

Behind the scenes, Jesse is deeply committed to preparation and detail. He believes that thorough planning and personalised attention mean your ceremony day runs smoothly and you can relax knowing you're in excellent hands. He takes the heavy lifting off your shoulders, allowing you to focus on what matters: celebrating your love story with the people who matter most.

Whether you're planning an intimate garden ceremony in Perth, a bold celebration in a regional location, or a destination wedding anywhere across Australia or internationally, Jesse brings the same professionalism, warmth, and expertise to every event. His 16+ years as a performer means he knows how to read a room, adapt on the fly, and deliver a ceremony that feels personal, polished, and perfectly suited to you both.

Jesse is available Australia wide and for international destination weddings. He works with couples who want a ceremony that feels natural, memorable, and genuinely theirs—one that their guests will talk about for all the right reasons.
