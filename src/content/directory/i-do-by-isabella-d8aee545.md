Izzy Jensen, trading as I Do by Isabella, is a Gold Coast-based marriage celebrant who specialises in creating relaxed, meaningful wedding ceremonies that feel completely and authentically you. With a warm, nurturing presence and genuine care for every couple she works with, Izzy brings calm guidance and thoughtful structure to one of the most important moments of your lives.

Izzy's journey to becoming a celebrant began unexpectedly when her best friend asked her to be part of her wedding ceremony. What started as a favour for someone she loved quickly evolved into a deep passion. She discovered that being a celebrant allowed her to do all her favourite things—speaking to a crowd, creating meaningful moments, and celebrating love. Her gift for connecting with people and her natural warmth make her the perfect guide to help you navigate your wedding day.

As your celebrant, Izzy's role is to craft a ceremony that reflects who you are as a couple, leaves your guests smiling (and perhaps tearing up a little), and sets the tone for an incredible celebration. She believes your ceremony should feel personal, heartfelt and completely aligned with your vision. From your very first chat through to the moment you walk back down the aisle, Izzy guides you through every step of the process—the legal paperwork, the ceremony script, the rehearsal and everything in between—so you can focus on enjoying your day.

Izzy specialises in crafting personalised ceremony scripts built around your real story, with the right balance of heart, humour and meaning. Whether you're planning a full ceremony with all the traditional elements, an intimate elopement for just the two of you, or a simple legals-only wedding, she'll make the whole experience stress-free, personal and exactly what you envisioned. Each ceremony is thoughtfully shaped around what matters most to you and your partner, ensuring that every word spoken feels authentic to your relationship.

Based on the Gold Coast, Izzy travels throughout the region and beyond to service couples across Brisbane, Byron Bay, the Sunshine Coast and the Gold Coast Hinterland. No matter where you choose to say your vows, she brings the same level of dedication, warmth and professionalism to your celebration.

Couples working with Izzy can expect a celebrant who is genuinely invested in their story, who listens carefully to their vision, and who provides expert guidance on the legal requirements of marriage in Australia. She creates an effortless, memorable experience that feels unmistakably yours—a ceremony that honours your love, respects your values, and creates a moment you'll remember for the rest of your lives.
