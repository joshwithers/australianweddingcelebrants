Carla Davern is a Commonwealth-authorised marriage celebrant, MC, and singer based in Sydney who brings genuine warmth, energy, and artistry to every ceremony she creates. With over 15 years of experience as a celebrant and nearly two decades as a professional entertainer, Carla has developed a unique approach to wedding ceremonies that blends heartfelt moments with joy and laughter—creating celebrations that feel authentically yours.

**A Celebrant Who Truly Understands Performance**

Carla's journey to celebrancy is deeply rooted in her love of performance. As a professional singer and entertainer for over 20 years, she built a successful career in the Australian music industry, developing the skills and confidence that now form the foundation of her celebrant work. This extensive background in live performance has given her an intuitive understanding of how to command a room, connect with an audience, and deliver moments that resonate emotionally and authentically. When she transitioned into celebrancy, it felt like a natural evolution—redirecting her passion toward what she describes as her greatest professional joy: helping couples celebrate their love story in front of the people who matter most to them.

**Creating Ceremonies That Reflect Your Love Story**

Carla's philosophy is straightforward: your ceremony should be a true reflection of who you are as a couple. She takes time to get to know your love story—how you met, what makes you laugh together, your values, and your dreams for the future. She then weaves these personal details into ceremonies that feel romantic, meaningful, and genuinely moving. Drawing on her performance expertise, Carla has a natural talent for pacing, timing, and delivery that keeps guests engaged and emotionally invested in your moment. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a large celebration, she brings the same level of care, creativity, and professionalism to every ceremony.

**Experience You Can Trust**

With involvement in over 1,500 weddings, Carla has worked with couples from all walks of life across diverse settings and styles. This depth of experience means she's prepared for whatever your day might bring—she knows how to handle unexpected moments with grace, keeps ceremonies moving smoothly, and creates an atmosphere where your love is genuinely celebrated. Her background as a professional performer also brings a level of polish and confidence to your ceremony, ensuring delivery that feels natural and authentic.

**What Couples Say**

Couples consistently describe Carla as warm, easy-going, bubbly, and kind. They appreciate her ability to put them at ease during the planning process and on the day itself, whether they're naturally confident in front of crowds or nervous about being the centre of attention. Her genuine warmth and approachable nature create a safe space where couples can be completely themselves. Testimonials highlight how she's attentive, proactive, and skilled at blending laughter with heartfelt moments, ensuring ceremonies feel both fun and deeply meaningful.

**Services Beyond Celebrancy**

Carla offers celebrant services, MC duties, and wedding music options. She can perform as a singer at your ceremony or recommend The Sydney Wedding Music Duo, an easy-going professional music ensemble offering an extensive repertoire across all genres. Whether you'd like live acoustic performances of your favourite songs or a special entrance piece, her music services elevate your ceremony to another level.

**Where You'll Find Carla**

Based in Sydney, Carla regularly services Sydney and surrounds, the Hunter Valley, and the Southern Highlands. She travels Australia-wide for couples looking for her unique blend of celebrancy and performance expertise. No matter where your celebration takes place, she brings the same energy, professionalism, and genuine passion to making your wedding day unforgettable.
