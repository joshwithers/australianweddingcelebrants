**Oh hey there!** I'm Nicky Surnicky. Yes, it's a rather unique surname and, no, my parents didn’t bestow it upon me—I married into it. But I assure you, I find it quite amusing and encourage you to share in the laughter.

**Once upon a time,** I grew weary of attending weddings where the ceremonies were, frankly, a bit of a yawn. I often found myself wishing they could be more entertaining and heartfelt. This realisation led me to create Marry Me Nicky in 2014, with the mission to craft ceremonies that are as engaging as they are unforgettable. My aim is to fill your special day with laughter and genuine emotions, ensuring that it’s memorable for all the right reasons.

**Although I’m based** in the beautiful Sutherland Shire of Sydney, I’m delighted to travel far and wide—from the bustling city of [Sydney](https://www.marrymenicky.com.au) to the scenic landscapes of Wollongong, the enchanting Kangaroo Valley, the charming Southern Highlands, and the majestic Blue Mountains. I work closely with couples who prefer the ease of planning everything from the comfort of their own homes.

**In my humble opinion,** and perhaps I am a touch biased, the ceremony should truly be the highlight of your wedding day. After all, it’s not merely about tying the knot; it’s about doing it in a manner that is genuine and unapologetically you. If you're searching for a [celebrant](https://www.marrymenicky.com.au) who can bring a touch of magic to your wedding, I’m here to help make your day extraordinary.
