Luke is the celebrant, MC, and DJ behind **Married By Luke** — a wedding services business built on the belief that your big day should feel authentically *you*, not like it's been pulled from a template.

Based in Brisbane and servicing weddings across Queensland from the Gold Coast through to Gympie (including the Sunshine Coast, Toowoomba, and everywhere in between), Luke specialises in creating unforgettable, highly personalised wedding experiences that truly reflect each couple's unique love story and vibe. His approach is refreshingly simple: take the time to genuinely understand who you are as a couple, what matters most to you, and then bring that to life in a way that feels effortless, natural, and unmistakably yours.

## A Three-in-One Wedding Professional

What sets Luke apart is his ability to wear multiple hats seamlessly on your wedding day. As your **Marriage Celebrant**, he's committed to crafting a ceremony that captures your relationship's essence — one that's heartfelt, cinematic in its storytelling, and delivered with just the right touch of humour and authenticity. He takes pride in the legal and personal aspects of his role, making the paperwork feel manageable and even enjoyable, so you can focus on the excitement of getting married.

As your **MC**, Luke guides the flow of your reception with the ease of someone who genuinely knows you and your people. He doesn't just announce the speeches and cake cutting — he weaves together anecdotes about you, your partner, your bridal party, and your families in a way that keeps guests engaged, laughing, and feeling part of something special. His hosting style blends seamless transitions with moments of warmth and connection, ensuring your day flows naturally from beginning to end.

As your **DJ**, Luke sets the party atmosphere that makes your dance floor come alive. He understands that music is about more than just playing songs — it's about reading the room, building energy, and creating the soundtrack to one of the biggest moments of your lives.

## The Married By Luke Experience

Luke describes himself as "young blood, old soul" — a vibe consultant who genuinely cares about getting to know his couples. From your first meeting, the process feels less like working with a service provider and more like catching up with a good friend who happens to know exactly how to make your wedding brilliant.

His commitment to you starts at the beginning of your wedding planning and continues right through to the final song on the dance floor. He's there to help craft and perfect every detail of your celebration, ensuring that the entire experience — from the moments before you walk down the aisle to the last dance — reflects who you are as a couple and feels genuinely yours.

Couples consistently describe working with Luke as easy, professional, and deeply personal. His ability to blend heartfelt moments with engaging energy, to weave humour and cheekiness throughout the day, and to create seamless transitions means your wedding day doesn't just happen — it flows beautifully and feels present.

## What to Expect

Whether Luke is standing with you at the altar, guiding your reception, or behind the decks, everything he does is centred around **connection** — between you and your partner, between you and your guests, and between all of you and the moment itself. He specialises in creating days where you feel truly present, your guests feel genuinely involved, and the entire celebration feels effortless.

If you're looking for a celebrant who goes beyond the standard script, who takes the time to understand your story, and who brings energy, professionalism, and heart to every aspect of your wedding day, Luke would love to meet you and be part of one of the biggest moments you'll ever share — giving you the best possible start to married life.
