I’m Luke, the face behind Married By Luke - and my approach to weddings is simple: it should feel like you, not a template.

I take the time to truly understand your story, your dynamic, and what actually matters to you, then bring it to life in a way that feels effortless, natural, and genuinely you. Whether I’m standing with you as your celebrant, guiding the flow as your MC, or behind the decks as your DJ, everything I do is centred around connection - between you, your people, and the moment itself.

Over the years, I’ve worked with couples who want more than just a “copy and paste” wedding. My style blends heartfelt, cinematic storytelling with a bit of humour, seamless flow, a touch of cheekiness, and dance floors that just slap.

It’s about creating a day where you feel present, your guests feel involved, and the entire experience flows - from the moment before you walk down the aisle to the last song on the dance floor.

It’d be an honour to meet you and the love of your life - and be part of one of the biggest moments you’ll ever share, giving you the best possible start to married life.
