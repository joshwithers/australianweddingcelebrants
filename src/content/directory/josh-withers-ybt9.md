Josh Withers is one of Australia's most experienced and travelled marriage celebrants, based in Hobart, Tasmania. Since 2009, he has officiated over 2,900 weddings across all Australian states and territories, and in more than 50 countries worldwide—from Europe and North America to Asia and New Zealand. His reputation for crafting meaningful, joyful ceremonies has earned him recognition in prestigious publications including the New York Times, Vogue, Marie Claire, and Hello May, making him a sought-after choice for couples seeking something authentic and genuinely memorable.

Josh's approach to ceremony design is fundamentally different. He writes every ceremony entirely from scratch—no scripts, no recycled paragraphs, no generic templates. Instead, he believes your ceremony should be a one-time, sacred conversation about your love, your values, and your vision for marriage together. Through relaxed conversations over coffee or video call, Josh takes genuine time to understand your story as individuals and as a couple. He explores what matters most to you, what you want your guests to know about your relationship, and how you envision your marriage unfolding. The result is a ceremony that feels authentically and uniquely yours, woven through with the specific details and moments that make your relationship special.

Central to Josh's philosophy is the belief that your ceremony should be the best part of your wedding—not something to 'get through' before the celebrations begin. He sees the marriage ceremony as the moment you breathe your marriage into existence, the firestarter to your whole celebration, and the anchor that grounds everything that follows. This ceremony-first mindset means every word, every story, and every moment is crafted with intention and care to create something truly meaningful for you as a couple and memorable for your guests.

On your wedding day, Josh delivers with the calm, confident presence and professional polish that matters. Drawing on a background in breakfast radio and television, combined with his natural warmth and engaging storytelling ability, he speaks for approximately 18 minutes—inspired by the TED Talk format. This length is long enough to be substantial and meaningful, yet short enough to keep everyone present and connected to what matters most. Critically, he delivers without reading from notes or scripts, creating an intimate, direct connection with you and your guests throughout the entire ceremony.

Professional audio is a cornerstone of Josh's service. He brings professional-grade Bose speakers and Rode microphones to every wedding, ensuring every word is heard clearly and distinctly by your guests. This attention to audio quality also means your videographer receives pristine, broadcast-quality audio for your wedding film, capturing the ceremony perfectly for years to come.

Josh is legally authorised to solemnise marriages in Australia under the Marriage Act 1961, and is also licensed in the USA and Canada. He handles all legal requirements professionally and straightforwardly, guiding you through the Notice of Intended Marriage process at least one month before your wedding. Whether you prefer to meet in person or via video call, Josh makes the legal side clear and simple so you can focus on the emotional and personal aspects of your ceremony.

He works with flexibility and refreshing directness built into his planning process. You'll have as many conversations as you need—no unnecessary meetings or homework assignments, just genuine connection and transparent communication. Quick replies, clear guidance, and no vendor kickbacks. His calm, confident presence adapts authentically to the moment, the people, and the beautiful chaos that is a real wedding celebration.

Based in Hobart, Tasmania—a place he considers his favourite part of the world—Josh regularly travels across Tasmania to Launceston and the Huon Valley. He travels Australia-wide and is available for ceremonies in Sydney, Melbourne, the Gold Coast, and beyond. Josh also specialises in destination weddings globally and co-leads The Elopement Collective with his wife, Brittany, offering couples intimate and meaningful wedding experiences worldwide.

Couples consistently praise Josh for creating ceremonies that feel like warm, authentic celebrations filled with honesty and joy, tailored to reflect exactly who they are as a couple. If you're looking for a celebrant who genuinely celebrates your story, delivers with professionalism and real presence, and believes your ceremony should be unforgettable—Josh Withers is worth meeting.
