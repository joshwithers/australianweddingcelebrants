Josh Withers is one of Australia's most sought-after marriage celebrants, renowned for creating personalised, unscripted ceremonies that couples describe as genuinely unforgettable. Based in Hobart, Tasmania—which he considers his favourite part of the world—Josh has solemnised over 2,900 weddings since 2009, travelling across all Australian states and territories and more than 24 countries worldwide. His distinctive approach to ceremony design and his ability to craft meaningful celebrations filled with warmth, honesty, and joy have earned him recognition in prestigious publications including the New York Times, Vogue, Marie Claire, and Hello May, establishing him as a trusted choice for adventurous couples seeking authenticity and genuine connection.

**A Fundamentally Different Approach to Your Ceremony**

Josh's philosophy is refreshingly clear: your ceremony should be the best part of your wedding, not something to 'get through' before the celebration begins. He sees the marriage ceremony as a sacred moment—the firestarter to your whole celebration, the anchor that grounds everything that follows. Every ceremony is written entirely from scratch, with no scripts, no recycled paragraphs, and no generic templates. Your marriage deserves more than 'insert couple here,' and Josh treats it as exactly what it is: a one-time conversation about something sacred.

Through relaxed, genuine conversations over coffee or video call, Josh takes time to understand your story as individuals and as a couple. He explores what truly matters to you—what you want your guests to know about your relationship, your values, your vision for marriage together, and how you imagine your future unfolding. This deep listening forms the foundation for a ceremony that feels authentically and uniquely yours, woven through with the specific details, moments, and stories that make your relationship special. There are no unnecessary meetings or homework assignments, just clear communication and transparent connection focused on bringing your vision to life.

**Delivery With Presence and Polish**

On your wedding day, Josh delivers with the calm, confident presence and professional polish that genuinely matters. Drawing on a background in breakfast radio and television, combined with his natural warmth and engaging storytelling ability, he speaks for approximately 18 minutes—inspired by the TED Talk format. This length is long enough to be substantial and deeply meaningful, yet short enough to keep everyone present and connected to what matters most. Crucially, he delivers without reading from notes or scripts, creating an intimate, direct connection with you and your guests throughout the entire ceremony, speaking authentically as though having a genuine conversation about your love and your marriage.

Professional audio is a cornerstone of Josh's service. He brings professional-grade Bose speakers and Rode microphones to every wedding, ensuring every word is heard clearly and distinctly by your guests, even in challenging outdoor venues. This attention to audio quality also means your videographer receives pristine, broadcast-quality audio for your wedding film, capturing the ceremony perfectly for years to come. Combined with his calm presence and ability to adapt authentically to the moment, the people, and the beautiful chaos of a real wedding celebration, Josh creates ceremonies that feel both emotionally resonant and professionally polished.

**Flexible Planning and Legal Authority**

Josh is legally authorised to solemnise marriages in Australia under the Marriage Act 1961, and is also licensed in the USA and Canada. He handles all legal requirements professionally and straightforwardly, guiding you through the Notice of Intended Marriage process at least one month before your wedding—whether you prefer to meet in person or via video call. The legal side is made clear and simple so you can focus on the emotional and personal aspects of your ceremony. As many conversations as you need are included, conducted over coffee or video call, with no unnecessary meetings or homework assignments.

**Availability and Specialisation**

Based in Hobart, Josh regularly travels across Tasmania to Launceston and the Huon Valley, and is available for ceremonies Australia-wide in Sydney, Melbourne, the Gold Coast, and beyond. He specialises in destination weddings globally and co-leads The Elopement Collective with his wife, Brittany, offering couples intimate and meaningful wedding experiences worldwide. Quick replies, clear guidance, and no vendor kickbacks define his approach to working with couples. Whether you're planning an elopement, an intimate gathering, or a larger celebration, Josh brings the same level of care, authenticity, and professionalism to every ceremony.
