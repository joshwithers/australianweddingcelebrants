**Josh Withers** is a highly sought-after marriage celebrant based in Hobart, Tasmania, renowned for crafting personalised and memorable wedding ceremonies. With over 17 years of experience, Josh has officiated weddings across every Australian state and territory, Europe, North America, Asia, and New Zealand. Thousands of couples from more than 50 countries have entrusted him with celebrating their love and commitment since 2009.

Josh is celebrated for his unique, unscripted approach that focuses on genuinely celebrating the couple's story and future rather than following traditional scripts. His couples consistently praise him for creating ceremonies that feel like a warm, authentic celebration—filled with honesty and joy, tailored to reflect who they are as a couple. His engaging storytelling abilities, honed from a background in breakfast radio and television, combined with his friendly and calm personality, make him a sought-after choice for couples seeking a heartfelt and entertaining ceremony.

Authorised under the Marriage Act of 1961, Josh is legally authorised to solemnise marriages in Australia, the USA, and Canada. He travels Australia-wide and is available for international destination weddings. Josh also leads The Elopement Collective with his wife, Brittany, a global elopement planning company specialising in intimate and meaningful wedding experiences. His media recognition includes features in the New York Times, Today Show, Vogue, Marie Claire, and Hello May.
